Hello, I'm a research scientist at the Oak Ridge National Laboratory involved in various modeling, simulation, and programming activities. I currently work on remote instrument control, machine learning, and large language model applications. Previous areas of research include fluidized bed reactors, battery cells and battery packs, rotor dynamics, water treatment, and biomass pyrolysis. I am also creator of the Numerix Swift package, Un1ts macOS app, Chemics Python package, BioComp web app, and the Genja static website generator. I am the former organizer of the KnoxPy Python users group and the Knoxville CocoaHeads group for iOS and macOS developers. See the sections below for my Notes, Software, Presentations, and Publications.

Contact me via Email or Mastodon. Donations to support my open-source work can be made through GitHub Sponsors, Patreon, Buy Me A Coffee, or PayPal.
