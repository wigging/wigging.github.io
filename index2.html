Gavin Wiggins

I am a research scientist at Oak Ridge National Laboratory near Knoxville, TN.

My current work is developing computational models (written in Python) to simulate the fast pyrolysis of biomass in fluidized bed reactors. This research is part of the Consortium for Computational Physics and Chemistry for the U.S. DOE Bioenergy Technologies Office. I am also involved with various battery simulation activities as part of the Computer Aided Engineering for Batteries project.

A list of my publications is available on Google Scholar. Several of my presentations can be downloaded as PDF documents from GitHub.

I am the developer of Un1ts and WeathrClip. I'm also the organizer of the Knoxville CocoaHeads and KnoxPy programming groups. Visit my GitHub profile to learn more about my coding projects and contributions to organizations.

Contact me via Email and Twitter. My full resume is available upon request.
